About Me
I love to wander and shoot people. Metro Manila based photographer Marjorie Maningding started taking photos professionally back in 2013. She had been photographing portfolios for models belonging to a
modeling agency he ran beginning 2014.
Seeing her work, a top Philippine
television network gave her her first paid
photography assignment to shoot
some of their young artists in 2015.
A new career began. Since then,
Marjorie Maningding has expanded her
craft to doing photography for
advertising, editorials, fashion, food &
beverages, products, people,
and corporations.
